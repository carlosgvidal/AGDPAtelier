Manifesto

The object as document. Weight as a form of truth.

A GROSS DOMESTIC PRODUCT does not manufacture accessories. The practice translates social theory and economic metric into material devices of high precision — physical essays that function as instruments of thought.

I — Vision

Vision

To establish conceptual infrastructure as the standard of contemporary luxury. The object is displaced from ornamentation toward the physical evidence of critical thought, consolidating an ecosystem in which pure metal functions as the final record of an intellectual vanguard. The ambition is to lead a niche of elite collecting in which value resides not in the aesthetics of shine, but in the gravity of form and the honesty of the object as historical and personal document.

II — Mission

Mission

To translate social theories and economic metrics into material devices of high precision. A GROSS DOMESTIC PRODUCT executes physical essays that operate as instruments of thought, integrating semiotics with rigorous structural engineering. The work exceeds decorative function to become statements of a radical aesthetic position — exposing the tension between individual sovereignty and global structures.

III — Promise

Promise

To deliver objects of undeniable technical solidity that function as anchors of identity and catalysts of reflection. The wearer is guaranteed a transmutation of jewelry into material document: an extension of intellectual rigor, grounded in the architecture of form and the inescapable presence of raw matter.

Values

Five principles govern the practice. None of them are negotiable.

01
Brutalist Rigor

Research precedes matter. The work does not manufacture accessories; it erects portable monoliths that compress intellectual trajectories into the density of metal.

02
Structural Gravity

Physical weight is a non-negotiable principle. Each piece is understood as an architectural construction for the body, in which technical precision eliminates all artifice or visual lightness.

03
Symbolic Tension

A reflective posture toward global systems is maintained throughout. Elevating macroeconomic indicators to the category of personal object generates a constant conflict between symbolic value and technical rigidity.

04
Honest Materiality

Metal is manifested in its most direct and pure state. Raw texture and industrial finish are prioritized as the definitive language, allowing form to communicate concept without traditional aesthetic mediation.

05
Conceptual Authority

The brand is the result of a practice in which aesthetics function as a form of knowledge. Value resides in the signature of an author who transforms thought into object, and the experience of language into tangible presence.

Positioning

The practice occupies the exact intersection between philosophical essay and authored jewelry.

01

Corporal Architecture

The territory is infrastructure, not jewelry. Technical excellence imposes a physical presence that uses weight as a language addressed to its surroundings.

02

Intellectual Legitimacy

Value emanates from the rigor of process. Integrating disciplines from economics and semiotics converts the object into a document of knowledge.

03

Subversion of Luxury

The name itself is a device of tension. Where the market sells fantasy, a cold economic metric is used to name the intimate.

Cultural Lineage

Affinities, not influences. The practice recognizes itself in three registers.

Cinema
Béla Tarr

The gravity of matter over the spectacle of image. Duration as a physical magnitude, rather than a narrative convenience.

Music
Iannis Xenakis

Structural logic as compositional method. Mass and density substitute for ornamental melody.

Literature
Alain Robbe-Grillet

The object as absolute physical presence, described through geometry and texture rather than sentiment.
