The Commission

Form is negotiated between intention, body and material consequence.

The object is not customized. It is authored in response.

Each commission begins as an exchange between the conditions brought by the client and the structural language of A GROSS DOMESTIC PRODUCT. The process does not reproduce an existing object with minor alterations. It establishes the premises from which a new object may emerge.

Scale, material, weight, typology and symbolic charge are considered together. The resulting work carries both the position of its author and the memory of the person who will carry it.

Process

Study precedes form. Execution follows agreement.

01 — Dialogue

Intention is defined.

The commission begins with context: the body, the occasion, the desired scale and the symbolic or structural conditions to be explored.

02 — Configuration

Constraints become structure.

Research, sketches and computational studies establish a precise formal system. The proposal is refined through controlled iterations.

03 — Execution

The object becomes matter.

Once the final configuration is approved, the work is produced, finished and documented as a unique object or strictly limited artefact.

Inquiry

Describe the conditions.
Not the solution.

A useful initial inquiry does not require technical drawings or a complete design. It should identify the territory in which the object will be developed.

01

The intended typology: ring, cuff, pendant, choker, tiara, ear object or another corporal structure.

02

The approximate material, scale or physical presence you wish to explore.

03

Any symbolic element, context, date or personal condition relevant to the commission.

04

Your preferred timeframe and delivery location.
